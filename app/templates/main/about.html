About Yale Trading Simulation

Yale Trading Simulation is an educational platform designed to help students learn about stock trading and investment strategies in a risk-free environment.

Our Mission

We aim to provide Yale students with hands-on experience in financial markets without the risk of losing real money. Our platform simulates real market conditions using live market data, allowing users to practice trading strategies, portfolio management, and investment analysis.

Features

  • Live market data and realistic trading simulation
  • Portfolio management and performance tracking
  • Social networking with fellow traders
  • Trading challenges and competitions
  • Educational resources and investment guides

Our Team

Yale Trading Simulation was developed by CPSC519 students at Yale University as a final project. Our team is passionate about finance, technology, and education.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, feedback, or suggestions about Yale Trading Simulation, please feel free to contact our team at yuntian.liu@yale.edu.

Disclaimer

Yale Trading Simulation is an educational tool and not a real trading platform. No real money is involved, and performance on this platform does not guarantee similar results in real-world trading scenarios. Always consult with a financial advisor before making real investment decisions.
