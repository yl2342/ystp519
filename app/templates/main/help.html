Help & Documentation

Getting Started with Yale Trading

Creating Your Account

Sign up with your Yale Net ID and get authenticated by Yale CAS to create an account. Each new user starts with $1,000 in virtual currency to begin trading.

Navigating the Dashboard

Your dashboard displays:

  • Portfolio summary and performance
  • Market indices and trends
  • Recent transactions
  • Social activity from other traders
Searching for Stocks

Use the search bar at the top of the page to find stocks by company name or ticker symbol.

Trading Stocks

Placing Orders

To buy or sell stocks:

  1. Search for a stock or select from trending stocks
  2. View the stock details page
  3. Enter the number of shares you want to buy/sell
  4. Review and confirm your order
Order Types

Currently, Yale Trading supports market orders that execute immediately at the current market price.

Trading Hours

Trading is available 24/7 in our simulation, unlike real markets that have specific trading hours.

Portfolio Management

Viewing Your Portfolio

Access your portfolio from the dashboard or the Portfolio tab to see:

  • Stocks you currently own
  • Purchase price and current value
  • Profit/loss for each position
  • Overall portfolio performance
Managing Cash

Your available cash balance is displayed on your dashboard. This is the amount you can use to purchase more stocks.

Social Features

Following Other Traders

Connect with fellow traders:

  • Follow other users to see their trading activities
  • View their public posts and trading insights
Sharing Trades

Share your trades and insights with the community:

  • Post about your recent trades
  • Share your analysis and trading strategies
  • Comment on other users' posts
